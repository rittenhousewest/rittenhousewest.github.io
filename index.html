About

I do my best to understand elementary particles and what they get up to in a variety of environments. The reason I do this is because I love the study of Nature. I love physics so much. Also the Universe even though it is so big I can barely comprehend it. Current obsession: Diquarks. Still. But also, hadron-hadron collisions. I know more about fragmentation functions than I ever thought I would.
